What is a Flexbox?

A flexbox is the classifcation of boxes that is used with the purpose of organising your website. You can use flexboxes to your advantage on creating boxes, which sounds boring yes. However it can probably be deemed as the most important visual aid to your website. You can create these flexboxes through the style tags. They are described as:

<head>

<style>

.header {

*insert any style attribute here*

}

</style>

</head>
